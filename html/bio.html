About Me

Hello, my name is Muzabel. I'm a Software Engineer and Social Entrepreneur.

I am passionate about using agile/scrum methologoies to build applications using MongoDB, ExpressJS, ReactJS, NodeJS (the MERN stack). I also work with Django and PostgreSQL. I think of myself as an entrepreneurial leader - with the ability to combine creativity, ingenuity and detail to create web/mobile applications that can make an impact in people's lives!

Born in the Democratic Republic of Congo, I grew up in refugee camps in Tanzania and Kenya, where I spent a total of 21 years. My life experience as a refugee has helped me develop many great values, including resilience, cross-cultural understanding, and lifelong learning.
