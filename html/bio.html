About Me

Hello, my name is Muzabel. I'm a Software Engineer and Social Entrepreneur

A graduate of General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive bootcamp, I am passionate about using agile/scrum methologoies to build applications using MongoDB, ExpressJS, ReactJS, NodeJS (the MERN stack), as well as Django and PostgreSQL. I am a lifelong learner - always open and looking for the next coding language to put my hands!

I was born in the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC), but grew up mainly in refugee camps in Tanzania and Kenya, where I spent a total of 21 years. I think of myself as an entrepreneurial leader - with the ability to combine creativity, ingenuity and detail to create web/mobile applications that can make an impact in people's lives!
